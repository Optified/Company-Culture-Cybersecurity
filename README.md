# Company-Culture-Cybersecurity

This paper talks about how company culture works, and the intrecacies involved when wanting to change a company culture to integrate cybersecurity practices.

In this example, cybersecurity in tech companies are not fully adopted within the culture, and many company practices are not the best security-wise. These improper practices could lead to huge costs in both financial and reputational damage through data breaches and lack of trust in company products down the line.

The goal of this paper is to teach why it's important that company culture is the most foundational way to integrate cybersecurity within a company.
